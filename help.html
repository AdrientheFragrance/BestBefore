A guide on how to use our service


Step 1: If you want to save the items in your pantry sign up to create an account or log in to access your existing account.

Step 2: Add ingredients to your pantry in the sidebar by typing them into the input box. Make sure to include their expiration date or date of purchase!

Step 3: Find out if your ingredients are near expiration and click on it to view recipes including that ingredient. Good luck preparing your meal!
