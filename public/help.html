A guide on how to use our service


1. Add ingredients to your pantry

Type them into the input box and click "add ingredient". Make sure to include their expiration date!


2. Check ingredient expiration

Determine if your ingredients are near expiration in the pantry. It is currently in the "list" view so click "calendar" to get the expiration calendar view.


3. Browse and manage ingredients

On the list view, select an ingredient and click "find recipes" to browse recipes including that ingredient. You can also delete the ingredient by clicking the "x".


4. Flip recipe cards

Click anywhere on a recipe card to flip it and view the required ingredients.


5. View detailed recipes

Click "view recipe" to navigate to a page with detailed recipe instructions.


6. Save and access recipes

Save or unsave recipes by clicking the bookmark icon. Access saved recipes by clicking "saved recipes".


7. Browse random recipes

At the bottom of the page, scroll horizontally to browse random recipes. Refresh the page to load different random recipes.


8. Enjoy your meal!
